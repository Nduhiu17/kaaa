General Meetings

We have two classes of general meetings – Annual General Meetings (AGMs) and Special General Meetings (SGMs)..

The AGM shall be held not later than the last Friday or last working day of February in each and subsequent year.

A SGM may be called for a specific purpose by the committee. Notice in writing of such meeting shall be sent to all members not less than 7 days before the date thereof and where practicable by Press advertisement, electronic mail or posted on the Association’s website .
